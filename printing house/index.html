Our Story

We would like to introduce ourselves as one of the foremost “On Demand” printing providers in Sri Lanka and the 22nd printer in the world to be awarded the prestigious FOGRA Certification, from Germany. The company was incorporated in June 2012 under the Ranpath Group of Companies, which has been in operation for over 20 years and engages in the processing of paper in the industry of printing. Our product portfolio and state-of-the-art production line, which includes the HP Indigo 12000, helps Corporates, SMEs and Exporters reduce their printing budgets considerably.

We pride ourselves in being environmentally conscious by only using FSC (Forest Stewardship Council) certified paper and Eco-friendly Inks in all our production processes.
